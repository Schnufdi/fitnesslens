Non-Negotiables
This is not a list of things you should give up. It is an honest assessment of the things you won't — and a clear-eyed view of what each one costs or contributes to your specific goals. Some will be fine. Some will have a trade-off worth knowing. A few might genuinely be working against something that matters to you. The system tells you which is which, and why, without moralising about it.
How the assessment works
Green — fine as-is. No meaningful trade-off with your goals.
Amber — trade-off exists. Worth knowing. Worth considering. Your call.
Red — genuinely working against a specific goal you have stated. The mechanism is explained. An alternative is offered.
The assessment is generated by the AI coach using your full profile — goals, knee status, nutrition journey, training phase. It updates if your profile changes.
